About Us

History

Founded in 2008, Durham House of Pets is a large specialty pet retailer of pet products.

At Durham House of Pets, we love pets, and we believe pets make us better people.

This vision impacts everything we do for our customers, the way we support our associates.

Durham House of Pets provides a broad range of competitively priced pet food and products.

Why use us

Here at Durham House of Pets we are an independent pet specialist company and always have your animals needs as our main priority. A far cry from your average pet shop experience, staff at Durham House of Pets are passionate animal lovers with a wealth of knowledge and expertise in niche areas.

Thanks to its independent status, we are able to stock a range of food rarely found elsewhere including Biologically Appropriate Raw Food.

We actively encourage owners to bring their pets along, especially when it comes to fitting collars, harnesses, coats and more, to make sure it is the perfect fit every time.
