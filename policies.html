Policies

Financing

Due to our size we are able to buy on the best terms available and pass on that benefit to our customers by offering the keenest prices on the Internet.

We check competitors' pricing regularly to ensure this is the case.

Delivery

We offer free shipping on all orders $49 and over in the lower 48-states. PO Boxes Excluded.

There is a flat shipping rate of $4.95 per order below $49. So go ahead and add that extra bag of treats to your order - you'll save the $4.95 shipping cost.

Return Policies

Return any product for any reason within 60 days of receipt for a refund equal to the amount paid for the product (not including shipping charges).

The refund will be issued back to the original method of payment.
